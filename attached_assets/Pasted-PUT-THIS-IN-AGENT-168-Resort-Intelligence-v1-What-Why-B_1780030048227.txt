#168 - Resort Intelligence v1

What & Why

Build a new admin page at /admin/resort-intelligence focused on destination demand, planning intent, and social coordination around resorts.

This is not a resort management page.

This is a market intelligence page.

Goal:

Answer:

- Which resorts are attracting interest?
- Which resorts convert interest into planning?
- Which resorts create social activity?
- Where is demand forming?

Use existing production data only.

Do not add:

- New database tables
- New analytics vendors
- New tracking systems

Before implementation, audit feasibility and report findings.
