FINAL SORTING LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------

Sort friends using the following priority, in order:

1) Newly connected friends first
   - A friend is considered "new" if the friendship was created
     within the last 7 days.
   - New friends should float to the top temporarily.
   - After 7 days, they follow normal sorting.

2) Friends with upcoming trips
   - Friends who have ≥1 upcoming trip come before friends
     with no upcoming trips.
   - Past trips do NOT count here.

3) Most recently added upcoming trip
   - Among friends with upcoming trips:
     sort by the most recent upcoming trip creation date (DESC).
   - This reflects recent activity, not total trip count.

4) Alphabetical by first name (A–Z)
   - Final tie-breaker for predictability.
