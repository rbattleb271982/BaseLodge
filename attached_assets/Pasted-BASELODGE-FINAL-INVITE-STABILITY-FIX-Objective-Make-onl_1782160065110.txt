## FIX 1 — RB-01

Find _connect_pending_inviter().

It currently still rejects pending friend invites if invite.is_expired() is true.

This is inconsistent with the permanent InviteToken policy and with the already-fixed GET/POST invite landing and confirm routes.

Change:

if not invite or invite.is_used() or invite.is_expired():

to:

if not invite or invite.is_used():

Do not change any other behavior in this function.
