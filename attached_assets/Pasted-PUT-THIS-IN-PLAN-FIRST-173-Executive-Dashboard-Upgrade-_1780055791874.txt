#173 - Executive Dashboard Upgrade

What & Why

BaseLodge now has multiple intelligence pages:

* User Intelligence
* Trips Intelligence
* Messaging Intelligence
* Activation Intelligence
* Growth Intelligence
* CRM & Lifecycle Intelligence
* Resort Intelligence
* Mountain Intelligence

The current Dashboard was built before most of these pages existed.

Goal:

Transform the existing Dashboard into the Executive Dashboard.

Do NOT create a new page.

Do NOT create a new route.

Upgrade the existing:

`/admin/dashboard`

This should become the first page a founder opens each day.

---

## GUIDING PRINCIPLE

A founder should not need to visit:

* Activation Intel
* Growth Intel
* CRM Intel

to understand business health.

The Dashboard should surface:

1. Business Health
2. Network Health
3. Activation Health
4. Reachability Health
5. Top Insights

Then allow drill-down into the deeper pages.
