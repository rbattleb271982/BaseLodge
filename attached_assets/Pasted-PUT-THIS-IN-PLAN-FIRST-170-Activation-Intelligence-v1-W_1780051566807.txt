#170 - Activation Intelligence v1

What & Why

BaseLodge now has:

* Working PostHog server delivery
* Activation events instrumented
* PostHog user property backfill completed
* Messaging Intelligence completed
* User Intelligence completed

We need a single place to understand how users move from signup to activation.

Goal:

Answer:

* Where do users drop off?
* Which activation steps are most commonly completed?
* Which activation steps are being skipped?
* How many users become active?
* Which activation milestones correlate with trips and friendships?
* Is activation improving over time?

Use existing production data only.

Do not add:

* New analytics vendors
* New databases
* New tracking systems
* New models
* New migrations

Reuse existing PostHog and BaseLodge user-state data.

Before implementation, perform a feasibility audit.

---

## PHASE 1 — FEASIBILITY AUDIT

Audit all activation signals.

Inspect:

1. signup_started
2. signup_completed
3. onboarding_completed
4. pass_added
5. availability_added
6. wishlist_added
7. trip_created
8. friend_connected
9. invite_generated

Also inspect user-state properties:

* has_completed_signup
* has_completed_onboarding
* has_pass
* has_availability
* has_wishlist
* has_trip
* has_friend_connection
* has_generated_invite

For each signal classify:

GREEN
Directly measurable today

YELLOW
Approximation available

RED
Not reliably measurable

Provide:

* Source
* Query approach
* Current counts
* Data quality concerns
* Recommendation

Do not build until audit is complete.
