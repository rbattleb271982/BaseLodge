BASELODGE — PRE-RELEASE APP QA AUDIT

Objective

Perform a comprehensive end-to-end QA audit of BaseLodge before the next iOS and Android build submission.

This is an AUDIT FIRST exercise.

Do not modify code, database records, configuration, notifications, deep links, templates, routes, models, or production behavior unless a critical blocker is discovered and explicitly documented.

The goal is to identify all defects, edge cases, UX issues, routing issues, deep-link issues, notification issues, and data integrity issues that could impact users.
