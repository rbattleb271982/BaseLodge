#194 - Onboarding Step 2 — Compact Vertical Layout

The new full-width card layout is working, but the screen is too tall and requires more scrolling than necessary on mobile.

Goal: reduce vertical height throughout Step 2 while preserving readability, tap targets, and the premium BaseLodge feel.

IMPORTANT

Do NOT change:

- Riding options
- Skill options
- Stored values
- Selection behavior
- Routes
- Models
- Validation
- Onboarding flow
- Header copy
- Progress indicator functionality
- CTA functionality
- Selected state styling
- Typography family
- Icons/images (do not add any)
- Emojis (do not add any)

Use checkpoints sparingly.

## Layout Optimization

Reduce vertical space wherever possible.

### Header Area

Tighten spacing between:

- Progress bar and back arrow
- Back arrow and STEP 2 OF 4
- STEP 2 OF 4 and headline
- Headline and subheading
- Subheading and first riding card

Move content upward where possible.

### Riding Cards

Reduce card height.

Specifically:

- Reduce vertical padding
- Reduce minimum height if currently oversized
- Reduce spacing between cards

Keep cards comfortably tappable on mobile.

Do NOT make them feel cramped.

Target:

- More compact
- More content visible above the fold
- Still easy to tap

### Skill Level Section

Reduce spacing:

- Between last riding card and SKILL LEVEL heading
- Between SKILL LEVEL heading and first skill card
- Between skill cards

Reduce skill card height similarly to riding cards.

### Continue Button Area

Reduce unnecessary whitespace above the Continue button.

Keep button usability unchanged.

Do not overlap content.

## Visual Requirements

Preserve:

- Full-width card layout
- Rounded corners
- Border styling
- Bordeaux selected state
- Existing helper text under None of the Above (Experience)
- Existing typography hierarchy

Do NOT add:

- Icons
- Emojis
- Trail symbols
- Images
- Decorative graphics

Text-only cards.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Noticeably less scrolling required on iPhone-width screens
- More of the skill section visible without scrolling
- Card heights reduced appropriately
- Vertical gaps tightened throughout the page
- Screen feels denser but not cramped
- Continue button remains fully usable
- No clipping or overlap
- No console errors
- Selection behavior unchanged
- Stored values submit correctly
- Onboarding completes successfully

Do not finish until you have tested on an iPhone-width viewport and confirmed the page is significantly more compact while maintaining usability.
