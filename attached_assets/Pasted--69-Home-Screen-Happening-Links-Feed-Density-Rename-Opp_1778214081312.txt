#69 - Home Screen: Happening Links + Feed Density + Rename Opportunities to Ideas

What & Why

Three visual and interaction improvements to the Home screen:

1. Happening rows are not tappable.
Each signal text (e.g. "Jonathan is going to Beaver Creek · 9 dates") is currently static text.
Since the backend already has the friend's user_id when building each signal, these rows should navigate to that friend's profile.

2. The Opportunities cards have more vertical padding than needed.
The “N today” eyebrow count adds unnecessary noise, and the cards themselves are slightly too tall for a dense mobile feed.

3. The Welcome/profile hero card has extra vertical bulk.
The gap between the equipment links section and the stats divider is looser than necessary.
The “Welcome, Richard.” headline at 26px is slightly oversized and risks wrapping on narrow devices.

4. Rename “Opportunities” to “Ideas”.
The current naming feels too productized/corporate. “Ideas” feels more social/editorial and pairs better with “Happening”.

Done looks like

- Every Happening row with a friend_id navigates to /friends/<id> when tapped.
- Happening rows show a subtle trailing chevron (›) to signal navigation.
- Rows without friend_id remain plain text safely.
- The Home section header now reads:
  “Ideas”
  instead of:
  “Opportunities”
- The “N today” count/eyebrow is removed entirely.
- Ideas cards feel visually tighter with slightly reduced vertical padding while preserving comfortable mobile tap targets (~44px minimum).
- The welcome headline font-size is reduced from ~26px → ~24px.
- The spacing between the equipment links and the stats divider inside the hero card is visibly tighter.
- Grouped Happening signals still work correctly.
- Home page loads cleanly with no regressions.

Out of scope

- Ideas ranking/data/logic
- Trips, Friends, or Profile backend logic
- Native iOS/Android files
- Changing Happening copy/grouping logic beyond adding links

Steps

In app.py inside the happening-signals builder (~6395–6430), include:

  - inherited text color
  - muted chevron

In:
templates/partials/home/_section_opportunities.html
