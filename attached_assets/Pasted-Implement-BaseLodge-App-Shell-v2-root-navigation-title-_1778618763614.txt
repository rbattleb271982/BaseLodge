Implement BaseLodge App Shell v2: root navigation title cleanup only.

Goal:
Now that BaseLodge has a fixed top bar, make the main root screens feel more native by moving their primary screen identity into the fixed top bar and removing redundant body H1/header rows.

Scope:
Root/peer-level screens only.
Do not touch detail screens yet.
Do not migrate back buttons yet.
Do not redesign screens.

Screens in scope:
- Home
- Trips
- Friends
- Mountains
- Invite
- Ideas
- Notifications

Desired top bar behavior:
- Home: top bar title should be “Welcome, [first_name].”
- Trips: top bar title should be “Trips”
- Friends: top bar title should be “Friends”
- Mountains: top bar title should be “Mountains”
- Invite: top bar title should be “Invite friends”
- Ideas: top bar title should be “Ideas”
- Notifications: top bar title should be “Notifications”

Top-right behavior:
- Default: keep initials avatar linking to profile
- Friends: move/preserve “Invite →” as the top-right action instead of the initials avatar, if safe
- Ideas: move/preserve “Edit dates” / “Add dates” as the top-right action instead of the initials avatar, if safe
- Do not force right actions if it creates layout risk

Implementation guidance:
- Add a simple per-page way for templates to define the fixed top bar title and optional right action.
- Keep this lightweight and Jinja-friendly.
- Avoid JS.
- Avoid route/model changes.
- Avoid touching messaging, push, database, or auth logic.
- Keep public/logged-out pages unchanged.

Remove redundancy:
- Home: remove the large duplicate “Welcome, [first_name].” body H1/header row from the home card, but keep the card content that still matters.
- Trips: remove the redundant large “Trips” body H1/header row.
- Friends: remove the redundant large “Friends” body H1/header row from both empty and populated states, but keep the Invite action.
- Mountains: remove the redundant large “Mountains” body H1/header row, but keep helper/subtitle copy only if it still reads naturally.
- Invite: remove the redundant large “Invite friends” / “Invite a friend” body H1 if the top bar carries that title, but keep the actual invite UI and explanatory copy.
- Ideas: remove the redundant large “Ideas” body H1 row, but keep the Edit/Add dates action and all idea content.
- Notifications: remove the redundant large “Notifications” body H1, but keep notification list/empty-state content.

Do NOT remove:
- tabs
- filters
- search bars
- empty-state explanations
- CTA buttons
- friend invite functionality
- trip cards
- mountain cards
- useful section labels like “Upcoming”, “Past”, “Happening”, etc.
- detail screen headers

QA:

Use checkpoints sparingly.
