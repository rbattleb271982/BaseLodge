--------------------------------------------------
GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

Make the Ideas tab a dense, scannable index:
- each item answers “is this worth tapping?” in ~1 second
- minimal vertical height (2 lines max for availability/trips)
- destination in headline
- dates always present for availability/trips
- pass always present (explicit phrasing)
- entire row tappable with a chevron on the right

Detail screens remain informational (no buttons for now).

--------------------------------------------------
DONE LOOKS LIKE
--------------------------------------------------

1) Feed rows/cards:
- NO text CTAs (“Plan trip”, “Join trip”, etc.)
- Entire row is tappable
- Subtle chevron on the right edge
- Availability + Trip cards are exactly 2 lines
- Wishlist is 1 line (can wrap naturally)

2) Metadata order on line 2 is always:
   Dates · People · Pass

3) Dates:
- ALWAYS shown on availability + trip cards
- Abbreviated format (e.g., “Jun 16–19”, “May 30–Jun 3”)
- Wishlist shows no dates

4) Names:
- If exactly 1 friend is referenced → FULL NAME (e.g., “With Tyler Grant”)
- If 2+ friends → count only (e.g., “With 4 others”)
- Do not use first-name-only or initials on the feed

5) Pass phrasing (ALWAYS present on availability + trip cards):
- 1 friend match → “You both have Ikon”
- multi-friend match → “All have Ikon”
- mismatch → “Different passes”
- Never use “Ikon Pass” / “Epic Pass”

6) Trip status:
- planning → “{Full Name} is considering {Destination}”
- going → “{Full Name} is going to {Destination}”

7) Detail screens:
- remain informational (NO CTAs)
- show dates, destination, people (full names), rider type + pass
- navigation works back to Ideas

8) Ranking (preserve and enforce):
- availability > planning trips > going trips > wishlist
- deterministic (not incidental)

--------------------------------------------------
CARD COPY (LOCK EXACT PATTERNS)
--------------------------------------------------

A) Availability (multi-person)
Line 1:
“A shared opening at {Destination}”
Line 2:
“{Dates} · With {N} others · All have {Pass}”
OR (mismatch):
“{Dates} · With {N} others · Different passes”

B) Availability (1 friend)
Line 1:
“A shared opening at {Destination}”
Line 2:
“{Dates} · With {Full Name} · You both have {Pass}”
OR:
“{Dates} · With {Full Name} · Different passes”

C) Trip (planning)
Line 1:
“{Full Name} is considering {Destination}”
Line 2:
“{Dates} · You both have {Pass}”
OR:
“{Dates} · Different passes”

D) Trip (going)
Line 1:
“{Full Name} is going to {Destination}”
Line 2:
“{Dates} · You both have {Pass}”
OR:
“{Dates} · Different passes”

E) Wishlist (single line, can wrap)
- 1 friend:
  “You and {Full Name} have {Destination} on your wishlist”
- 2+ friends:
  “You and {N} friends have {Destination} on your wishlist”

Do NOT use:
- “Join”
- “Saved” / “wishlisted”
- first-name-only
- pass shorthand like “Ikon” alone
- “Same pass” / “Passes align”
