Goal

Add lightweight trip planning helpers and profile signals that improve coordination and alignment without introducing social noise. These are intent-based signals, not logistics, comments, or feeds.

⸻

Scope

Trip Detail page (new fields + display)
Profile page (new optional signals)

No new navigation tabs.
No messaging, comments, or feeds.
No data model overhauls beyond adding the required fields.

⸻

1) Trip Planning Helpers (Trip-level)

These fields live only on the Trip Detail page.

A) Trip Basics

Add the following optional fields:
	•	Lodging area / base area
	•	Free text (e.g., “Staying near Lionshead”)
	•	Arrival day / departure day
	•	Simple selectors or inputs
	•	Used for context only (no logic attached)
	•	Dates flexible toggle
	•	Boolean: “Dates flexible”

Editing rules:
– Editable by trip owner only
– Optional (no required fields)

⸻

B) Transportation Signal

Add a single-select transportation intent field.

Options (exact copy):
	•	Driving
	•	Flying
	•	Public transportation
	•	Already there / local

Display rules:
– Display label uses lighter phrasing: “Getting there”
– Example: “Getting there: Public transportation”
– Intent-only (no routes, no addresses)

Editing rules:
– Trip owner only
– Optional

⸻

D) Day-level Availability

Add simple checkbox availability for days of the week relevant to skiing.

Default set:
	•	Friday
	•	Saturday
	•	Sunday

Rules:
– Optional
– Unchecked by default
– No calendar overhaul
– No auto-filling logic

Purpose: clarify overlap without requiring conversation.

⸻

Empty State (Trip-level)

If none of the above trip planning fields are filled out:

– Show a subtle inline prompt:
“Add trip details”

Do NOT show placeholder text or empty labels.

⸻

2) Profile Signals (Profile-level)

Add two optional profile attributes that help alignment.

C) Typical Ski Pace

Single-select, optional.

Options (exact copy):
	•	First chair
	•	Mid-morning
	•	Late starts

D) Après Interest

Single-select, optional.

Options (exact copy):
	•	Après-friendly
	•	Ski hard, head home

Rules:
– Optional now
– May be nudged later (not required during setup)
– Display only on the user’s own profile (not on friend profiles yet)
– Calm, secondary presentation

Purpose: signal vibe, not identity.

⸻

Display & Tone Rules (Global)
	•	These are signals, not primary content
	•	Use muted text and secondary hierarchy
	•	No icons or emojis
	•	No comments or reactions
	•	No notifications tied to these fields

⸻

Do NOT Do
	•	Do not add chat, comments, or feeds
	•	Do not surface these on list cards
	•	Do not add maps, routing, or logistics
	•	Do not make any of these required

⸻

Acceptance Criteria
	•	Trip Detail pages feel more helpful, not busier
	•	Friends can self-align without messaging
	•	Profiles feel more informative without becoming social profiles
	•	App remains calm, intentional, and coordination-focused
