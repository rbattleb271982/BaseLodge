#172 - CRM & Lifecycle Intelligence v1

What & Why

Growth Intelligence answers:

"Is the network growing?"

CRM & Lifecycle Intelligence should answer:

"Who should we message, who is at risk, and where should we intervene?"

Goal:

Answer:

* Who is reachable?
* Who is dormant?
* Who is activated?
* Who is engaged?
* Who is a power user?
* Which users are stuck?
* Which users need a nudge?
* Which lifecycle segments are growing?
* Which CRM audiences should exist today?

Use existing production data only.

Do not add:

* New analytics vendors
* New databases
* New tracking systems
* New models
* New migrations

Reuse:

* User
* Friend
* SkiTrip
* InviteToken
* MessageEventLog
* PushDeviceToken
* PostHog-backed user properties
* Existing lifecycle_stage data
