#27 - Ideas Feed V2: Final Compression Pass

Implement the final Ideas feed compression and copy system. This is a polish pass to lock layout, wording, and hierarchy.

Do not redesign. Do not add new features. Do not change routes or data models.

---

## GOAL

Make the Ideas feed:
- strictly 2-line for availability + trips
- single-line (wrapping) for wishlist
- fully tappable rows with a subtle chevron
- dense, scannable, and premium
- free of CTA text

---

## DONE LOOKS LIKE

- No CTA text anywhere in feed rows
- Entire row is tappable
- Subtle chevron (›) on right edge
- Availability + trip cards = exactly 2 lines
- Wishlist = one sentence, may wrap
- Metadata order is always: Dates · People · Pass (availability only)
- Trip metadata is always: Dates · Pass (no people)

---

## COPY SYSTEM

### Availability

Line 1:
"A shared opening at {Destination}"

Line 2:
"{Dates} · With {Full Name | N others} · {Pass phrase}"

---

### Trip (planning)

Line 1:
"{Full Name} is considering {Destination}"

Line 2:
"{Dates} · {Pass phrase}"

---

### Trip (going)

Line 1:
"{Full Name} is going to {Destination}"

Line 2:
"{Dates} · {Pass phrase}"

---

### Wishlist (single sentence only)

"You and {Full Name} have {Destination} on your wishlist"

or

"You and {N} friends have {Destination} on your wishlist"

No separate title/subtitle.

---

## NAME RULES

- If exactly 1 friend:
  → use FULL NAME
- If 2+ friends:
  → "With {N} others"
- Do not use first-name-only or initials

---

## PASS RULES

Always show pass on availability + trip cards.

Allowed phrases ONLY:
- "You both have {Pass}"
- "All have {Pass}"
- "Different passes"

Rules:
- If mismatch → ALWAYS "Different passes"
- Do NOT list multiple passes (no "Ikon + Epic")
- Do NOT use "Ikon Pass" or "Epic Pass"

---

## DATE RULES

Dates must be abbreviated:

Examples:
- "Jun 16–19"
- "May 30–Jun 3"

Do NOT use:
- full month names
- numeric formats (6/16)
- inconsistent dash styles

Dates must always appear for:
- availability
- trips

Wishlist has no dates.

---

## TEMPLATE RULES

- Remove all CTA text spans
- Add right-aligned subtle chevron (›)
- Ensure entire row is clickable
- Collapse to 2-line structure strictly
- Availability metadata: Dates · People · Pass
- Trip metadata: Dates · Pass (no people)
- Wishlist: single-line sentence

---

## STYLING

- Maintain comfortable tap targets
- Chevron must:
  - be right-aligned
  - be subtle (low contrast)
  - not affect layout

## DETAIL SCREENS

- Keep informational only
- Keep:
  - destination

- Add new UI elements
