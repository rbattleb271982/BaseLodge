────────────────────────────────────────
GOAL
────────────────────────────────────────
Prevent the iOS app icon badge from incrementing when a push notification arrives while the app is already open in the foreground.
Current issue:
AppDelegate.swift currently uses:
[.banner, .sound, .badge]
inside willPresent()
Because the app is already active:
applicationDidBecomeActive does not fire
the badge remains visible on the home screen icon
until next app resume/open cycle
Desired behavior:
foreground pushes still show:
banner
sound
BUT do not increment badge count
Background notifications should continue behaving normally.

ios/App/App/AppDelegate.swift
