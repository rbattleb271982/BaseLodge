Things we still need to figure out on the logic side
Before you move this to Agent, there are a few product decisions we should lock:
1. What is the primary unit?
We need to decide whether Ideas should show:
one row per date
one row per friend
or one featured best match plus secondary rows
My recommendation:
one row per date, because the action is planning a trip around a day/window, not around a person.
2. How many friend names do we show?
If 4 people overlap on the same day, do we show:
“Alex, Sam, and 2 others”
or list everyone
or just show a count
My recommendation:
Show 1–2 names max, then “and X others.”
3. What is the CTA destination?
Do we want the primary CTA to go to:
/add_trip with prefilled date and one friend
or a detail view for that overlap date
or just keep them on Ideas
My recommendation for now:
Go to /add_trip with prefilled start_date/end_date, and if needed include one friend as the anchor friend.
4. Do we want one “best match” pinned at the top?
You already have a “Best Match” on Home. We should decide whether Ideas should:
also feature the best one first
or just show a clean chronological list
My recommendation:
Use a featured top overlap, then a list of remaining overlaps below. That gives the page hierarchy.
5. What happens to the setup ladder once populated?
My recommendation:
Do not leave the full ladder sitting there once overlaps exist.
Instead:
collapse it into a compact “Keep improving your ideas” module
or remove it entirely and keep one subtle secondary CTA lower on the page
