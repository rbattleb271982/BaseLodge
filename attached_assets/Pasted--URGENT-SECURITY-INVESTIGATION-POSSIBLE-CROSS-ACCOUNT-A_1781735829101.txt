## Incident Description

User A ("Danielle") downloaded and opened the app.

When she first launched the app, she reports that she was automatically logged into User B's account ("Solo") without ever entering Solo's email address or password.

Danielle states:

- She has never had Solo's credentials
- Solo has never used Danielle's phone

- Danielle could see Solo's account
- Danielle then logged out and created her own account

Assume the report is accurate until disproven.

This would represent unauthorized access to another user's account and must be treated as a potential security incident.
