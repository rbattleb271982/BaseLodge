#171 - User Intelligence Dashboard v1

What & Why

Growth tells us how many users arrive.

Activation tells us whether they reach value.

Retention tells us whether they come back.

Product tells us what they use.

Messaging tells us how they respond.

User Intelligence tells us who they are.

Goal:

Answer:

- Who are our users?
- What passes do they hold?
- What resorts do they care about?
- What states do they come from?
- Are they skiers or snowboarders?
- What characteristics correlate with activation?
- What characteristics correlate with retention?

Use existing production data only.

Do not add:

- New tables
- New tracking
- New vendors

Before implementation perform a feasibility audit.
