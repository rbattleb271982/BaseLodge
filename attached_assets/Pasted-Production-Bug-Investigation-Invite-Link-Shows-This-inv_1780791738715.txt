Production Bug Investigation — Invite Link Shows "This invite has expired"

Context:

A user who originally had a BaseLodge account during the web-app beta period signed into the current mobile app and generated a fresh friend invite link.

The recipient opened the link immediately and received the "This invite has expired" page.

Important detail:
- The inviter and recipient were previously friends in BaseLodge during testing.
- The inviter generated the link from the current Invite tab.
- The recipient opened it immediately after it was generated.
- This is not an old saved link.
- The re-invite fix was recently deployed to allow users to reconnect after unfriending.
