Goal
Reduce perceived clutter across Home, Trips, and Friends by improving hierarchy, grouping, and readability. No new features, no redesign, no routing changes. This is spacing, hierarchy, and clarity only.

––––––––––––––––––
	1.	Collapse Equipment (Home / Profile card)
––––––––––––––––––

Default (collapsed):
Equipment: Volkl Mantra · Tecnica Mach1

On tap, expand inline (no modal, no animation):
Equipment: Volkl Mantra 177cm, Tecnica Mach1 130

Rules:
– Collapsed by default
– Smaller font than identity line
– Muted secondary color
– Inline expand/collapse only
– Editing remains in Settings → Equipment

If no equipment exists, show:
Equipment: Add equipment →

2) Group Stats into a Single Unified Row
––––––––––––––––––

Replace:
4 upcoming trips    5 mountains visited

With:
4 trips · 5 mountains

Rules:
– Entire row is tappable
– Same destination as previous “mountains visited” link
– Same font weight and color for both stats
– No partial underlines

––––––––––––––––––

Add a subtle horizontal divider inside the Home/Profile card separating:

Top (identity):
– Welcome, Name

Divider rules:
– Thin

– Comfortable vertical spacing

6) Trip Cards — Two Lines, No Emojis
––––––––––––––––––

Replace current single-line + emoji trip cards.

Line 1 (primary, bold):
Dec 31 – Jan 08 · Belleayre, NY

Line 2 (secondary, muted):
Pass: Other · Open to ski

Rules:
– Remove raised-hand emoji entirely
– Use explicit text labels (e.g. “Open to ski”)
– Use · separators, not commas or dashes
– Slightly increase internal vertical padding
– Keep cards compact (no height bloat)

Edit button:
– Keep “Edit” on the right
– Vertically centered
– No behavior changes

––––––––––––––––––
7) Friends List — Tighten Card Hierarchy
––––––––––––––––––

Replace current friend card layout with consistent two-line structure.

Line 1 (primary, bold):
Quinn Alpine · UT

Line 2 (secondary, muted):
Skier · Advanced · Epic

Rules:
– Name first, state abbreviated
– Rider type → skill → pass order
– Use · separators (no dashes)
– One line per row (truncate if needed)
– No orphaned words (e.g. “CA” alone on a line)

Layout & spacing:
– Reduce vertical padding slightly
– Ensure consistent left alignment
– List should feel tight and scannable, not profile-heavy

Typography:
– Line 1 weight matches trip card line 1
– Line 2 matches trip card line 2
– No mixed font sizes within a line

Do NOT change:
– Filter pills or counts (All / Epic / Ikon / Other)
– Tap behavior (card → friend profile)
– Sorting logic

––––––––––––––––––

– Equipment collapses and expands inline
