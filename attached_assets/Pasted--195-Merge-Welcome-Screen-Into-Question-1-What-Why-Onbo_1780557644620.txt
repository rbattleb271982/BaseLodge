#195 - Merge Welcome Screen Into Question 1

What & Why

Onboarding currently opens on a standalone welcome screen ("Welcome aboard. / Let's get started") before showing any questions. This adds a tap and a screen of friction without providing meaningful value.

Merge the welcome moment into Question 1 and reduce the onboarding flow by one screen and one tap.

Done looks like

- Onboarding opens directly on Question 1 (rider type + skill level)
- Above the Question 1 headline, this line appears:

  Welcome to BaseLodge. Let's get you set up.

- The Question 1 headline reads:

  What best describes you?

- Question 1 back button is hidden (no previous step)
- Progress bar shows 1 of 3 segments filled on Question 1
- Step counter reads:
  - Step 1 of 3 on Q1
  - Step 2 of 3 on Q2 (pass)
  - Step 3 of 3 on Q3 (location)
- All selection behavior remains unchanged
- Stored values remain unchanged
- Form submission remains unchanged
- No console errors
- Mobile spacing remains clean and balanced

Out of scope

- Changes to Question 2, 3, or 4 content
- Onboarding routes
- Validation logic
- Rider options
- Skill options
- Pass selections
- Location selection behavior

Steps

1. Delete the welcome panel

Remove the entire welcome step:

- div.ob-step containing:
  - ob-welcome-content
  - cta-1

Remove unused CSS:

- .ob-welcome-content
- .ob-welcome-logo
- .ob-welcome-title
- .ob-welcome-body

2. Fix slider geometry

Current:
- width: 400%
- step width: 25%
- slideTo uses (n - 1) * 25%

Change to:
- width: 300%
- step width: 33.333%
- slideTo uses (n - 1) * 33.333

3. Rebuild new Step 1 (former rider/skill step)

Change:

- id="ob-step-2" → id="ob-step-1"

Rename compact CSS overrides:

- #ob-step-2 → #ob-step-1

Back button:
- Add invis class
- Remove onclick="goBack(2)"

Progress bar:
- 1 segment active
- 2 inactive

Step counter:
- "Step 1 of 3"

Add this intro line directly below the step counter:

Welcome to BaseLodge. Let's get you set up.

Style:
- Small supporting text
- 14px
- Muted color
- Tight spacing

Headline:

What best describes you?

Replace:

Tell us about your riding.

Remove the subtitle entirely unless spacing feels too empty.

CTA:

- id="cta-1"
- onclick="goNext(1)"

4. Rebuild new Step 2 (former pass step)

Change:

- id="ob-step-3" → id="ob-step-2"

- #ob-step-3 → #ob-step-2

- Step 2 of 3

5. Rebuild new Step 3 (former location step)

Optionally add:

Back button:

- onclick="goBack(3)"

- 0 inactive

Step counter:

- Step 3 of 3

- id="cta-3"

- Welcome intro line displays correctly

templates/identity_setup.html
