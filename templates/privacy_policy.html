Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What we collect

BaseLodge collects the information you provide when you create an account — your name, email address, and ski trip preferences. We also collect the availability dates and trip details you add to the app.

How we use it

Your information is used solely to power the BaseLodge experience: matching your availability with friends, suggesting trips, and personalising your profile. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Sharing with friends

Availability overlap information is only surfaced to mutual connections. Your exact open dates are never shown to people who are not already your BaseLodge friends.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely in our cloud database. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

Contact

For any privacy questions, email us at hello@baselodge.app.
